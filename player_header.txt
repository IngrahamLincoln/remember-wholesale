Welcome to the neon future! In Cyberpunk 2020:
- Choose your background: Are you a savvy Fixer? A digital Netrunner? A devious Hacker?
- Based on your choice, you'll be given two skills to navigate this world.
- Describe your actions in detail. From negotiating with a street vendor to hacking into a mainframe, detail matters.
- Spend XP to level up your skills or acquire new ones tailored to your adventures.
- Engage in the narrative, shape the story, and make a name for yourself in the urban sprawl.
